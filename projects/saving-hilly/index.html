Summary

Saving Hilly is an immersive and educational AR game that instructs individuals on the importance of slope safety. Additionally, the game showcases state-of-the-art technology designed to help individuals deal with landslides. For more details, please find at https://slope-aoe.hkust.edu.hk/home.
